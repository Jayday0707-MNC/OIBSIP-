## 📖 About Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela was South Africa's first democratically elected President and one of the world's most respected leaders.

After spending **27 years in prison** for opposing apartheid, he became a global symbol of peace, reconciliation, democracy, and human rights.

His leadership helped unite South Africa and inspired millions around the world.

- First Black President of South Africa
